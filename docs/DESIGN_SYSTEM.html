Design Principles

The rules behind every decision.

Content First
The design steps back. Articles, headlines, and images are the product — not the interface.
One Accent
Rose crimson is used sparingly — nav, buttons, category labels. Repetition on a neutral background makes it powerful.
Trust Through Clarity
Sensitive health topics demand an approachable, trustworthy tone. Clean type, generous whitespace, no clutter.
Two Modes, One Brand
Light mode for reading. Dark Editorial for atmosphere. The rose accent and serif type unify both experiences.
